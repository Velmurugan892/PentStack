The Objective

Empowering Local Commerce

Bridging the Gap

Namma Area is more than just an app; it's a movement to digitize local commerce. Our objective is to facilitate the delivery of grocery items strictly from local small and wholesale stores. By eliminating the middleman, we empower local vendors and provide consumers with fresh, affordable goods.

We've focused majorly on covering all over Tamilnadu, including remote small villages that are often ignored by giant delivery chains. Technology should serve everyone, everywhere.
